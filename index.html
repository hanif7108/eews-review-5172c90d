TL;DR — Ringkas — The IDA-PTW operational framework achieves composite R² = 0.7091 across 103 spectral periods (oracle upper bound 0.7779) on 23,537 Indonesian accelerograms under event-grouped 5-fold CV. Stage 0 URPD (AUC = 0.9136) shrinks the near-field blind zone from 38 km to 11 km (Balanced, 100% Damaging Recall) / 4 km (Aggressive). Stage 1 achieves 79.64% overall / 81.68% balanced accuracy on an MMI-hybrid 3-class discretisation via XGBoost + LightGBM soft-voting ensemble with SMOTE and Optuna tuning. The 90-feature input set integrates rosers_ptw3 + prior site + Dai multi-window + frequency-domain features. 99.44% Golden Time Compliance. Retrospective validation on Cianjur 2022 & Sumedang 2024 yields 100% Stage-0 Damaging Recall. Submission-ready for IEEE Access.
