EXPERIMENT: Impact of Expanded Vs30 Data on Stage 2 Sa(T) Prediction
Author: Hanif Andi Nugraha (hanif.andi@ui.ac.id)

TIMELINE:
- Baseline (42 features, no Vs30): trained ~March 29, 2026
- Vs30-Old (203 stations): trained ~March 31, 2026  
- Vs30-Expanded (338 stations): trained April 10, 2026

DATA CHANGES:
- Old Vs30.csv: 203 stations with measured Vs30 values
- New METADATA_VS30.csv: 338 stations with measured Vs30 values (+135 new)
- 4 misaligned rows corrected (Vs30 in Class column)
- 265 stations in training data got updated Vs30 values
- 117,965 traces (60.6% of 192,525) affected

RESULTS COMPARISON:
                    Baseline(42f)  Vs30-Old(44f)  Vs30-Expanded(44f)

R² min                 0.4808         0.4983          0.5488
R² max                 0.6439         0.6811          0.7969
Δ vs Baseline            —           +5.14%          +24.60%

KEY FINDINGS:
- 100% of 103 spectral periods improved (103/103)
- Short period (T=0.05-0.6s): ΔR² = +0.175 vs Old
- Long period (T≥3.0s): ΔR² = +0.130 vs Old
- PGA: R² 0.533 → 0.696
- Sa(1.0s): R² 0.550 → 0.705

NOTE: 404/515 models evaluated with holdout fold-1 (not full OOF).
Full OOF retraining recommended for manuscript-quality numbers.

DECISION: KEEP Vs30-Expanded as production model set.
